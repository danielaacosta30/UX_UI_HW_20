USDA Website Redesign

A Government Website Redesign

The United States Department of Agriculture website, like most government websites, suffers from information overload and a lack of consistent hierarchy. Their website seeks to serve a diverse community of agricultural professionals, but finding key information can be difficult at times.

The Problem

The United States Department of Agriculture website, like most government websites, suffers from information overload and a lack of consistent hierarchy. Their website seeks to serve a diverse community of agricultural professionals, but finding key information can be difficult at times.

The Solution

Analyze the existing website for pain points, perform user testing, and identify the key resources & purpose. Use this research and the application of UI best practices to perform a full redesign of the USDA website.
